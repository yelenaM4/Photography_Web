Want to see some amazing photos taken by photographers around the world ?

You will find 3 categories of photography

  1. Portrait

    • Traditional portrait: In a traditional portrait, the person is usually staring at the camera and posing for the photographer. Typically, these are shot in a studio with a formal photography backdrop. These portraits are frequently cropped to display only the subject's head and shoulders rather than the entire body.

    • Lifestyle photography: is a type of photography that focuses on capturing pictures of people in ordinary situations, real-life events, or milestones in an artistic way. The major purpose is to communicate personal experiences or to inspire people at various times.

    • Environmental portrait: An environmental portrait is a portrait taken in the subject's ordinary setting, such as their home or job, and it usually focuses on their life and surroundings. The term is most commonly used to describe a type of photography.

    • Street portraits: are a sub-genre of street photography; nothing is contrived or posed in street photography. You normally ask complete strangers whether you can photograph them for urban street photos.

  2. landscape

    Landscape photography is the skill of photographing nature and the outdoors in such a way that your spectator feels immersed in the subject.

  3. Wildlife

    Wildlife photography is a type of photography that focuses on capturing different types of wildlife in their natural surroundings.
